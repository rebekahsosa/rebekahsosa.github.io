CASE STUDY

CHALLENGE

Create a new feature for Impossible Foods, a Silicon Valley start-up disrupting the food industry. Create a product roadmap to launch and assess the solution.

PROJECT INFO

Role: Lead UX & UI Designer

Skills: Sketch, Craft, & InVision

Date: Nov. 15, 2016

Location: Miami

View Prototype

SOLUTION

Impossible Foods is expanding to restaurants nationwide, but not available in every city. Users are not currently able to receive a notification when the Impossible Burger is available in their city. Users will be provided with two opportunities to sign up for a newsletter to receive notifications regarding product availability. The first option will be integrated into the city locator page A newsletter modal will also appear on the homepage for a specified amount of time.



RESEARCH

Competitive research, quantitative analysis, user interviews, card sort, personas and journey maps.

KEY INSIGHTS
  • The Impossible Burger is currently available in restaurants only.
  • The company is using social media and celebrity chefs to drive attention to restaurants serving its product.
  • Key customer segments include the following: meat-lovers, health and dietary conscious, vegetarians, food enthusiasts, vegans, and environmentally conscious.
  • Competitors are actively collecting user information to send notifications when products are available locally.

My passion for human-centered design began several years ago during an internship with CHOICE Humanitarian, an international NGO working to end extreme poverty. I spent 4 months in a remote area of Kenya where communities faced serious issues such as drought, lack of education and insufficient healthcare. I observed the local CHOICE Kenya team as they walked the community through the human-centered design process and supported them as they began to design solutions to some of their toughest challenges.

I’ve worked with CHOICE for over 8 years now and I’ve experienced the power human-centered design has to bridge cultures and drive toward innovative solutions. I currently hold a BA in Spanish and I am a graduate student in CU Boulder’s MA for Strategic Communication Design.
